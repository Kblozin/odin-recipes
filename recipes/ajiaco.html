Ajiaco

Ajiaco is a hearty Colombian soup

Known for its rich flavors and variety of ingredients

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Potatoes (creole, white, and Andean papas criollas)
  • Corn
  • Herb called guascas
  • Avocado
  • Capers
  • Cream
  • Rice (optional)

Steps

    • Prepare the chicken broth
    • Add potatoes
    • Include corn
    • Introduce guascas, a traditional Colombian herb that gives ajiaco its distinctive flavor.
    • Shred chicken
    • Prepare accompaniments, Slice the avocado and set aside. Drain and rinse capers if they're preserved in brine. Also, prepare the cream for garnishing
    • Serve ajiaco
    • Garnish, Serve the ajiaco with a side of rice, slices of avocado, a spoonful of capers, and a dollop of cream.
    • Enjoy!
